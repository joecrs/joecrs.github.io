How much can you earn?

We pay up to 80% of scrap yard value for your items.

Why you would use this service?

  1. No cost to you
  2. Earn money
  3. Less mess
  4. Spend more time focusing on your business and earning a profit
  5. Lower costs for trash removal or other associated costs
  6. Can be a more consistent and efficient way to handle your scrap

How it works for you

  1. We pick up your scrap at no cost to you
  2. We pay you based on your preference of either
    1. price per pound, paid immediately at time of pickup
    2. 80% of scrap value, paid after processing (typically two weeks)
