We are your business partner in recycling. We can maximize your return on your recyclable materials, specializing in recycling HVAC equipment

What we do:

  1. We pick up your scrap for free ($20 fee applies for quantities under 1000lb)
  2. Clean materials (wire, aluminum radiators, motors) are valued at market price
  3. Whole condensers are broken down and the components (coil, compressor, etc) are valued at market price
    - Breakdown fee of 20% condenser value (remaining value still higher than before breakdown)
    - Freon recovery fee of $20 if required
  4. We pay you!

We recycle a variety of equipment:

  • Condensers
  • Air Handlers
  • Motors
  • Compressors
  • Boilers
  • Pumps
  • And many more!
  • Roof top Units
  • Window AC units
  • Contactors
  • Wire
  • Water Source Heat Pumps
