We are your business partner in recycling. We can maximize your return on your recyclable materials, specializing in recycling HVAC equipment

What we do:

  1. We pick up your scrap for free ($20 fee applies for quantities under 1000lb)
  2. Clean materials (wire, aluminum radiators, motors) are valued at our scrap price (see chart)
  3. Whole condensers are broken down and the components (coil, compressor, etc) are valued at our scrap price
  4. We pay you within 10 business days!

We recycle a variety of equipment:

  • Condensers
  • Air Handlers
  • Motors
  • Compressors
  • Boilers
  • Pumps
  • And many more!
  • Roof top Units
  • Window AC units
  • Contactors
  • Wire
  • Water Source Heat Pumps

  4. Stops employees from fighting over scrap
