We are your business partner in recycling. We can maximize your return on your recyclable materials, specializing in recycling HVAC equipment

What we do

  1. We pick up your scrap ($20 fee applies for quantities under 1000lb)
  2. Clean materials (wire, aluminum radiators, motors) are valued at market price (see below)
  3. Whole condensers are broken down and the components (coil, compressor, etc) valued at market price
    • Breakdown fee of 20% condenser value
    • Freon recovery fee of $20 if applicable
  4. We pay you

What We Recycle

We recycle a variety of equipment for which you can receive compensation:
  • Condensers
  • Air Handlers
  • Motors
  • Compressors
  • Boilers
  • Roof top Units
  • Window AC units
  • Pumps
  • Contactors
  • Wire
  • Water Source Heat Pumps
  • And many more!
